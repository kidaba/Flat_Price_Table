ABOUT US

InspiredWeb.co.za was created to provide affordable but stunning web design services to startup businesses. Our focus is not just on creating websites but on helping small businesses use their websites effectively in order to build an online presence. We want to ensure that our websites become powerful marketing tools for our customers. We are based in Randburg, Jhb but work with clients from all around South Africa.

WHAT WE DO

We believe that website creation, Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) and online advertising go together. We help you with all 3. Our sites work on all devices and are mobile friendly.

WEBSITE & LOGO DESIGN

A great looking website is the first step in creating an online presence. We work closely with you to ensure that potential customers find the information they need on your website. All websites are responsive and work on all phones and tablets.

SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMISATION

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) is the process of getting search engines to add your site to their search results. We will create your website so that it is optimised for search engines from the start based on keywords that you would like to be associated with. We will then give you information on how to grow your online presence.

ONLINE ADVERTISING

We will provide you with a report of Google searches around your product or service and help you understand how to use Google and Facebook advertising.

OUR DESIGN PROCESS

We constantly strive to improve the way we work and to ensure that our customer are 100% happy with our service. Our web design\development process follows the three steps below. We aim to complete a first draft of your website within a week.

UNDERSTAND YOUR BUSINESS

We work closely with you to understand your business to ensure we give you a solution that fits your needs.

DESIGN WEBSITE

We then send you a mock up of the website to get input. Once we have this we develop the website.

TEST WITH YOU

We test the website with you until you are satisfied with the final product.
